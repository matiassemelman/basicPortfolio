How do I get here?

8 years of work experience in different areas. From a call center where I learned to deal with people, I later worked as an Asian imports coordinator while studying International Trade at the Universidad del Salvador.


The country took a protectionist stance and I decided to move to the sales area, where I started as a sales analyst and ended up as a sales coordinator with 10 salesman in charge, leading the company to 16 consecutive months of sales growth.


At the beginning of 2021, I decide to start studying programming, at a slow pace since I was working long hours. Today I know that I discovered my passion, what I can do for fun, for the simple fact of improving myself and solving problems. Thank you for reading me, and do not hesitate to contact me.
